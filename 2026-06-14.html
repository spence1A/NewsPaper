Reported June 2 in JCO Oncology Practice and presented at the 2026 ASCO meeting, a retrospective study of about 110,000 women with excess weight found those taking GLP-1 medications — the class behind Ozempic and Wegovy — were up to 30–35% less likely to develop breast cancer.
