Scientists are scrambling to update formation theories, because the standard models simply can't produce what JWST is observing. The discovery is the latest in a string of JWST findings that are quietly dismantling textbook planetary science.

A New Registry Is Mapping Who Actually Uses Differential Privacy

A collaborative research team has launched the Differential Privacy Deployments Registry — a public database tracking which companies and government agencies are actively using differential privacy (a mathematical technique that lets you learn from data without exposing individuals). The initiative was born from frustration that DP was being claimed widely but deployed rarely. Apple, Google, and the US Census Bureau are among the verified users. As AI data hunger grows, knowing which platforms actually protect their training data mathematically is becoming a meaningful trust signal.
