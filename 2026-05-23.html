California Advances Two CCPA Reform Bills — Limiting Data Sales and Expanding Deletion Rights

Separately, New York's S 2539 passed the state Senate requiring retailers to post visible notices when collecting biometric data from customers via electronic devices — a first-in-the-nation mandate.
